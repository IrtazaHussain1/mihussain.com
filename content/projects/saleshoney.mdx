# What is Saleshoney?
Saleshoney specializes in lead generation, leveraging AI to create highly effective campaigns for clients. We start with a comprehensive consultation to gather your campaign objectives and identify target market segments. Within the first week, you'll receive a curated list of contacts and access to our web app, alongside insights into our snippet creation process. By the end of the first month, we assess campaign performance to refine our targeting strategy. We continuously iterate and refine our process weekly, experimenting with your Total Addressable Market (TAM) and providing regular reports to optimize your marketing efforts and drive ongoing success.
